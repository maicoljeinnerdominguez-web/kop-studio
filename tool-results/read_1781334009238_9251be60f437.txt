   108→### Architecture
   109→- **SPA approach**: All views render on `/` route, client-side navigation via Zustand

   111→- **API**: 7 REST endpoints under /api/
   112→- **Database**: SQLite with Prisma ORM
   113→- **Styling**: Dark theme, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui components
   114→

   116→- Announcement bar with marquee animation

   127→### Unresolved Issues / Risks
